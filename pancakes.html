Pancakes

A soft, fluffy breakfast favorite that's quick to make and perfect with maple syrup, honey, or fruit.

🥣 Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Butter
  • Maple syrup (optional)

📋 Recipe Information

⏱ Preparation time
🍽 Serves
⭐ Difficulty

20 mins

2 people

Easy

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Mix the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the egg and milk.
  3. Stir until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly buttered pan.
  5. Pour the batter.
  6. Flip when bubbles appear.
  7. Serve warm.

💡 Tip

Don't press the pancakes with a spatula. It makes them less fluffy!

🍯 Best served with

  • Maple Syrup 🍁
  • Fresh Strawberries 🍓
  • Blueberries 🫐
  • Whipped Cream 🍦
