🍜 Ramen

Ramen is a comforting Japanese noodle soup made with a flavorful broth, springy noodles, and a variety of delicious toppings. Warm, satisfying, and easy to customize, it's a favorite meal enjoyed around the world.

🥣 Ingredients

  • 1 pack ramen noodles
  • 2 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • Sweet corn
  • Spring onions
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Black pepper
  • Chili flakes (optional)

📋 Recipe Information

⏱ Preparation time
🍽 Serves
⭐ Difficulty

20 mins

2 people

Easy

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Bring the broth to a gentle boil.
  2. Add the mushrooms and corn, then cook for a few minutes.
  3. Stir in a little soy sauce, sesame oil, and black pepper.
  4. Add the ramen noodles and cook until tender.
  5. Pour the ramen into a bowl.
  6. Top with the boiled egg and chopped spring onions.
  7. Sprinkle chili flakes if desired and serve hot.

💡 Tip

Don't overcook the noodles. They should stay slightly firm for the best texture.

🍽 Best served with

  • Gyoza 🥟
  • Iced Tea 🧋
  • Kimchi 🥬
