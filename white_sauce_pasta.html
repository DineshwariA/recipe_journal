🍝 White Sauce Pasta

White Sauce Pasta is a creamy Italian-inspired dish made with tender pasta coated in a rich, cheesy white sauce. It's simple to prepare, comforting, and perfect for a cozy lunch or dinner.

🥣 Ingredients

  • 200 g pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Mixed vegetables (optional)

📋 Recipe Information

⏱ Preparation time
🍽 Serves
⭐ Difficulty

30 mins

2 people

Easy

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta according to the package instructions and drain it.
  2. Melt the butter in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Stir in the flour and cook for about a minute.
  4. Gradually add the milk while stirring continuously.
  5. Add the cheese, salt, black pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  6. Mix in the cooked pasta and vegetables.
  7. Cook for another 2 to 3 minutes and serve hot.

💡 Tip

Keep stirring while adding the milk to make the sauce smooth and creamy without lumps.

🍽 Best served with

  • Garlic Bread 🥖
  • Fresh Salad 🥗
  • Lemon Iced Tea 🍋
