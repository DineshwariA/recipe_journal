🍰 Chocolate Cake

Chocolate Cake is a rich, moist dessert loved for its deep chocolate flavor and soft, fluffy texture. Perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or simply satisfying a sweet craving, it's a timeless favorite enjoyed by people of all ages.

🥣 Ingredients

  • 1 and a 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Chocolate frosting (optional)

📋 Recipe Information

⏱ Preparation time
🍽 Serves
⭐ Difficulty

50 mins

8 people

Medium

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda in a bowl.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Mix until the batter is smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  6. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes.
  7. Let the cake cool before adding chocolate frosting.

💡 Tip

Let the cake cool completely before frosting so the icing stays smooth and doesn't melt.

🍽 Best served with

  • Vanilla Ice Cream 🍨
  • Fresh Strawberries 🍓
  • Hot Coffee ☕
  • Whipped Cream 🍦
