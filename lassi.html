🥛 Lassi

Lassi is a refreshing Indian yogurt-based drink that's smooth, creamy, and perfect for hot days. Traditionally served chilled, it can be enjoyed sweet, salted, or flavored with fruits and aromatic spices, making it a delicious and cooling beverage.

🥣 Ingredients

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup chilled water
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Ice cubes
  • Chopped pistachios (optional)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

📋 Recipe Information

⏱ Preparation time
🍽 Serves
⭐ Difficulty

10 mins

2 people

Easy

👨‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Add the yogurt, chilled water, sugar, and milk to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and frothy.
  3. Add the cardamom powder and blend for a few seconds.
  4. Pour into serving glasses.
  5. Add ice cubes if desired.
  6. Garnish with chopped pistachios and saffron.
  7. Serve chilled.

💡 Tip

Use chilled yogurt for the creamiest texture, and adjust the sugar to suit your taste.

🍽 Best served with

  • Samosa 🥟
  • Kachori 🥙
  • Paratha 🫓
